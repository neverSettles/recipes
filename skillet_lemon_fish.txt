To cook Skillet Lemon Fish:

Use Olive oil on pan, heat on medium heat
Prepare 4, 4oz fish filets (4 * 4 oz = 16 oz = 1lb), place directly in heated pan

Sprinkle salt, Cajun powder, Lemon Juice, Fresh Lemons on first side of fish

Flip Fish, sprinkle toppings again.

Cook for about 5 minutes on each side on Medium-high heat.
